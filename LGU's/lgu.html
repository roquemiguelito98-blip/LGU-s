LGU's municipalities of malolos bulacan

Malolos, as a component city in Bulacan, has a rich history and a diverse range of municipalities. Here are some of the key municipalities within Malolos:

Barangay Anilao:

Known for its historical significance and community activities.

Barangay Atlag:

A peaceful community with a focus on local governance.

Barangay Bagna:

Engaged in various community projects and initiatives.

Barangay Bagong bayan:

A community that values its heritage and development.

Barangay Balayong:

Known for its agriculture activities and community spirit.

These municipalities contribute to the vibrant and dynamic nature of Malolos, each with its own unique characteristics and contributions to the city's overall development.
